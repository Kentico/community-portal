# Kentico / Xperience by Kentico Glossary

Correct term, then common dictation mishears. Add to this list as new mishears
turn up; it's the main thing that improves accuracy over time.

## Product names

- **Xperience by Kentico** — Experience by Kentico, expedience by Kentico, X
  Prairie by Kentico, experience by kinetico
- **XbK** — X B K, ex bee kay, ex-be-kay
- **Kentico Xperience 13 / KX13** — Kentico experience thirteen, k x thirteen,
  KX 13
- **KentiCopilot** — Kentiko pilot, Kentico co-pilot, Kenny co-pilot, Kentiko Co
  Pilot
- **AIRA** — Ira, Iyra, A I R A, Ayra
- **Kentico MVP** — Kentico M V P, Kentico movie (misheard "MVP")
- **Kentico Community Leader** — Kentico community leaders, community lead

- **Headless** — headless, head less
